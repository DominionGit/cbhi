Sign Up

Are you interested in volunteering on a medical mission or non-healthcare program, experience to serve poor and rural communities in Nigeria while at the same time doing what you love to do by helping people in need or even gaining a broader experience and building unique and lasting relationships? Or, are you interested in our Medical Care Internship and non-Healthcare Volunteer program for foreign medical and allied science students as well as others from the United States, Canada and Europe? If so, then you have arrived at the right place. Or are you just interested in being one of our volunteer fundraisers from wherever you live? Please sign up! Complete and submit this form, and a representative from Capacity Bay Health Initiative will contact you soon.

CapacityBay Health Initiative (CBHI) is a not-for-profit healthcare and humanitarian relief agency which focuses on and specializes in medical and emergency relief services in rural communities
